```
Husband dead. 
Herself, alive. 
Children half a world away.

The mothers of her homeland stand appalled. 

Avyaan cooks cake for an agonarch
and comforts herself for the choice; 
at least he is kind to his wife —

— and feeds her euphorigum 
to break down 
the pain of exile.
```
